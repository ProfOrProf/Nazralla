Ki11ahB

Wikiprofia article • Twitch

Ki11ahB is a Canadian Twitch streamer and content creator known for his speedruns and hitless challenge runs in the Soulsborne genre of games. He gained notoriety in the hitless community for his highly optimized gameplay, signature comedic style, and dedication to the craft of challenge running.

Career

Ki11ahB began streaming on Twitch in the late 2010s, rising to prominence in the Soulsborne and FromSoftware speedrunning communities. His breakthrough moment came with a notable Bloodborne speedrun in which he completed the game in 00:41:04[1]. Despite a few minor mistakes, the run was widely considered to be "basically sub 41 minutes" and solidified his reputation as a top-tier runner.

His completed no-hit runs include:

  • Dark Souls – No Hit Any%
  • Dark Souls – SL1 All Bosses + DLC
  • Dark Souls II – No Hit Any%
  • Dark Souls III – No Hit Any%
  • Bloodborne – No Hit Any%[2]
  • Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice – No Hit Shura (No Prosthetic)
  • Demon’s Souls – No Hit Any%
  • Elden Ring – No Hit Any%

Ki11ahB is known for balancing mechanical precision with a lighthearted, often irreverent streaming persona.

Cultural Impact

One of Ki11ahB’s most defining contributions to the hitless scene was a nearly 40-hour period in which he wore mutton chops. This era, now referred to in niche circles as The Chop Epoch, is regarded as a turning point in hitless gaming history[3].

He is also known for the catchphrase "Come to Canada!"[4].

Personal Life

Ki11ahB is in a relationship with muse Juulnifer, who once laughed at one of his jokes. This singular moment of comedic validation sparked the (still unreleased) creative project often referred to as his comedy album.

Legacy

While still early in his career, Ki11ahB has left a lasting impression on the hitless and speedrunning scenes. His blend of elite gameplay, chaotic energy, and understated Canadian charm continues to inspire a new generation of runners.
