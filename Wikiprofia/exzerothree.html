exzerothree

Wikiprofia article • Twitch

exzerothree (exze) is a Twitch streamer and content creator known for hitless challenge runs in the Soulsborne genre of games. She is best known for completing Elden Ring hitless with only shields[1], counting bats to raise awareness for bat conservation, and loving cats more than any other Team Hitless member—maybe anyone, ever.

Career

exze began streaming on Twitch in the 2020s, quietly rising to prominence in the hitless community with no cam/no mic streams farming run after run in Elden Ring. She ran exclusively on console (PS5) until 2025. She shocked Team Hitless with a voice reveal in 2024 and a face reveal in 2025. Everyone previously assumed her to be a cat.

Her completed no-hit runs include:

  • Elden Ring – No Hit Any%
  • Elden Ring – RL1 No Hit Any%
  • Elden Ring – No Hit AGR
  • Elden Ring – No Hit AR (base game)
  • Elden Ring – No Hit Any% Serpent Hunter Only
  • Elden Ring – No Hit Any% No HUD (WF)[2]
  • Elden Ring – No Hit Any% Sorcery Only
  • Elden Ring – No Hit Any% Ringed Finger Only
  • Elden Ring – No Hit Any% Incantations Only
  • Elden Ring – No Hit Any% Shield Only (no damaging consumables)
  • Bloodborne – No Hit Any%
  • Bloodborne – BL4 No Hit Any%
  • Sekiro – No Hit Any% (Shura)

exze is known for helping to develop a script for Malenia[3] and for popularizing a comfy scripted stagger strat for Godfrey/Hoarah Loux[4].

Cultural Impact

One of exze’s defining contributions to the hitless scene was popularizing the Soulless Trilogy—Bloodborne, Sekiro, and Elden Ring back to back without taking a single hit. The name "Soulless" was coined by Dr.Doot, but it wasn’t until exze started yapping about it that runners began to take it seriously as an alternative to the traditional Trilogy (Dark Souls I–III).

Although she has not yet completed the Soulless Trilogy herself[5], she still hopes to someday do so.

Her pink kitty emotes are ubiquitous, and her stream playlist is also well known in the community for being full of goth/EBM/industrial bangers[6].

Personal Life

Despite exze’s professed love of cats, she is unable to have any of her own due to allergies.

Legacy

exze works tirelessly in service of Team Hitless and to help new runners achieve their dreams. She is also dedicated to raising awareness about animal welfare and conservation, including cat-related programs like TNR (trap-neuter-return).
